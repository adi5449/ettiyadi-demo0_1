Who We Are

Weaving Tradition
With Modern Elegance

Founded in 2012, Ettiyadi Weddings began as a single planner's dream — to create celebrations that honour Indian heritage while embracing contemporary design sensibilities.

Today, we are a team of 30+ passionate professionals — designers, coordinators, stylists, and storytellers — united by one purpose: making your most important day flawlessly beautiful.

From opulent Maharashtrian ceremonies to minimalist beach weddings, we bring equal passion and expertise to every celebration we touch.
